How to Use the App

Getting started with 'The Unlabeler' is as simple as taking a photo. Follow these easy steps to transform your perspective-distorted labels into perfect digital memories:

  1. Select or Take a Photo
    • On the first screen, take a photo of your bottle or chose one from your gallery.
    • Ensure the label is clearly visible and as large as possible against a contrasting background.
    • If possible, use a telephoto zoom and not a wide-angle lens to avoid additional distortion.
    • This helps the app to accurately process and transform the label.
  2. Mark the Label
    • On the next screen, quickly tap to mark the four corners and (roughly) the center of the top and bottom edges of the label.
    • You can drag the image with one finger and zoom in or out with two fingers or the zoom buttons to see the details.
    • For precision, use the magnifying glass at the bottom to get a closer view of each point.
    • Position the top left corner of the label in the magnified area and tap to mark it.
    • Then, mark the center of the top edge and the top right corner. Repeat this process for the bottom edge.
    • The sequence doesn't matter – just ensure all six points are marked accurately.
  3. Adjust the Marks
    • If you need to correct a mark, simply tap the eraser button to delete the last mark you placed.
    • Alternatively, you can tap on a specific mark to select it and then press the eraser button to remove it.
    • This feature ensures that you can make adjustments easily without starting over.
  4. Unlabel the Image
    • Once all marks are set, press the "unlabel" button.
    • The app will perform a quick calculation to correct the perspective distortion.
    • Within moments, you will see the transformed label displayed on the next screen.
  5. Add a Star Rating, Save or Share
    • If you want you can add a star rating to the label by clicking on the star button, tapping on the empty stars for a rating, choose your color scheme and add it.
    • Now you can save the unlabelled image to your phone by pressing the down arrow button, which stores it directly in your gallery.
    • You can also share the perfectly rectangular label with other apps using the share icon, making it easy to add to your tasting notes or digital collection.
    • If you want to unlabel another picture, tap the plus icon to start the process again.
    • Should any marks need further adjustment, simply tap the back button (chevron in the top left corner) to refine them.

With 'The Unlabeler', capturing perfect label shots has never been easier. Transform your bottle labels into flawless digital images and share them with ease. Whether you're adding to your tasting notes, digital collections, or just sharing with friends, 'The Unlabeler' ensures the memories of your favorite drinks look their best.
