In-App Purchases

Free Version

With the free version of 'The Unlabeler' you can unlabel and save as many labels as you want, but a small watermark will be added to all labels. Just test it out and you will be convinced!

Pure Labels

With the "Pure Labels" in-app purchase you can enjoy all your future labels without a disturbing watermark.

Sommelier Package

For power users, there is the „SOMMELIER PACKAGE“ which unlocks a variety of advanced features like:

  • Saving your labels in a separate album,
  • Using a customizable name for shared labels,
  • Setting a fixed pixel size for all labels, so they have the same width in your tasting app,
  • Zooming in on the final label to check its quality,
  • Adding a star rating to the final label, and more.

All in-app purchases are one-time purchases. No subscriptions or hidden fees.

The app works on iPhone, iPad and Mac with the same in-app purchases.
