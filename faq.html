FAQ

Here are your frequently asked questions:

  1. Are there restrictions for the form of the label and the bottle?

    Yes, the Unlabeler App can correct the distortion of rectangular labels on cylindrical bottles, which applies to most standard bottles.

  2. How can I remove the watermark from the labels?

    To remove the watermark, please upgrade to the pro version via an in-app purchase. The watermark will be removed, allowing you to enjoy perfect label pictures. You can find the purchase option on the settings page.

  3. Is there a dark mode for the app?

    Yes, absolutely. Give it a try and enjoy the new look!
